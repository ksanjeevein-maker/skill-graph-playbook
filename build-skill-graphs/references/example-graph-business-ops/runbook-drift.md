# Runbook Drift

Use this note when the documented workflow and the real workflow have slowly diverged.

Runbooks drift when teams route around reality without updating the written guidance. Read [[handoff-boundaries]] when the problem is ownership confusion, and use [[queue-health-versus-root-cause]] when the symptoms show up first as performance or backlog issues.

## Key Rule

If people consistently bypass the runbook, fix the system or fix the document, but do not pretend they still match.

## Inputs

- repeated exceptions
- undocumented manual steps
- gaps between stated and real ownership

## Outputs

- whether to update the graph
- whether to update the operational source of truth
- whether the issue is structural or temporary
