# Triage Severity

Use this note to separate urgency from mere visibility.

A task is not automatically urgent because it is loud, recent, or affecting an executive. Follow [[queue-health-versus-root-cause]] when the problem looks broad but the evidence is still shallow, and move to [[handoff-boundaries]] when the right outcome is ownership transfer rather than faster local handling.

## Key Rule

Severity should reflect business impact, customer reach, and time sensitivity together.

## Inputs

- affected customers or systems
- duration or deadline pressure
- business criticality

## Outputs

- initial severity judgment
- first routing decision
